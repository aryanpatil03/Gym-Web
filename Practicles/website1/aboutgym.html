Our Vision

We created Cloud9 to change the way the fitness industry works. We’re committed to a new, collaborative model that innovates at every step. We’re setting new standards for service and satisfaction each and every day. We’re on a journey to reinvent fitness. Let’s take it together.

Our Mission

To provide quality instruction in a clean and professional environment while keeping things fun and enjoyable! Regardless of skill level whether you are a complete beginner or a seasoned professional, we have programs and classes that will suit your needs. We make it a priority to ensure that every student feels part of an empowering community and is able to grow and develop in a healthy learning environment..

Customized programs
